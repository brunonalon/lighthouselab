SHRIMP RISOTTO

Ingredients

  • 250g Arborio rice
  • 1 kg medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves chopped garlic
  • 1 tomato sauce can
  • 2 grated parmesan packets
  • 1 heavy cream can
  • 1 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • juice of 2 lemons
  • 3 cups reduced-sodium shrimp broth
  • Best server with a good white wine
Tips

Preparation

  1. Wash the shrimp and let it soak in the juice of 2 lemons for about 15 minutes
  2. Fry the onion and garlic in butter until golden brown
  3. Garlic should be placed shortly after the onions do not burn
  4. Add the shrimp dripping excess lemon juice and cook
  5. With the cooked shrimp place the tomato sauce, the seasoning cubes dissolved in 4 tablespoons of hot soup and mushrooms
  6. Let the sauce ascertain
  7. Mix the rice with this sauce, stir well
  8. Place the grated cheese and stir some more
  9. Finally add the cream
  10. To serve garnish sprinkling parsley
